About Us

More than just a clan - we're a family of competitive players

Our Mission

Founded in 2022, Nexus Vanguard brings together competitive and casual Valorant players who value teamwork, improvement, and sportsmanship. We strive to create a positive gaming environment while competing at the highest levels.

Clan Structure

  • Clan Founder: PhantomShot
  • Team Captains: 3
  • Main Roster: 5 players
  • Academy Team: 5 players

Regions

  • North America (Primary)
  • Europe (Secondary)
  • Asia (Developing)

Expectations

  • Minimum rank: Gold 3
  • Active 3+ times per week
  • Positive attitude
  • Willingness to learn

Requirements

  • Microphone required
  • Discord membership
  • Participation in scrims
  • Minimum age: 16
